Overview

Purpose

To present the legal services of Cristina Vera, an experienced attorney based in Milagro, Ecuador. The site allows users to view services, schedule appointments, submit documents for identity verification, and access legal resources in both Spanish and English.

Content

Home

The homepage introduces Cristina Vera and her areas of legal expertise. It includes a video presentation, call-to-action button for consultations, and bilingual support.

Images for the Home page

Services

This page outlines the services offered: family law, women's rights, childhood and adolescence, domestic violence, notarial services (contracts, powers of attorney), and transit cases. It also includes identity verification via ID upload.

Images for the Services page

Contact

This section contains a contact form, ID upload, and Google reCAPTCHA. It also provides direct contact information and a link to schedule consultations via Calendly.
